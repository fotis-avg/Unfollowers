Unfollowers Privacy Policy

Unfollowers is designed to protect your privacy. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

The app does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or diagnostic information. It has no backend servers, user accounts, or third-party analytics services.

Network Access

The only network activity performed by the app is an optional anonymous check against Instagram's publicly available profile endpoint to determine whether a profile exists. This request does not transmit any user data or personally identifiable information beyond the public profile name being checked. No information from these requests is stored or shared by the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

Your Privacy

Because the app does not collect or retain personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete.
